# **Taxonomy**
## **Brand Safe**
**Definition:**
Completely **G-rated, universally appropriate, and workplace-safe.** Absolutely no sexual content, cues, or interpretations possible. No violence or harm themes. Corresponds to Wattpad Rating: Everyone.
### Okay - Illustrated (Okay_Illustrated)
- Overall tone is positive or neutral, supporting a safe and inclusive environment.
- Content is non-sexual, non-violent, and non-graphic.
- No sign of self-harm.
- No depiction of illegal activities, harmful behaviour, or unsafe scenarios.
- Clothing and presentation are modest and appropriate for a teen audience (both real and illustrated). Similar to what you would see on a school website or youth program.
- No discriminatory, hateful, or bullying imagery toward an individual or group.
- No Personal identifying information displayed (eg. addresses, school IDs).
- Couples could be holding hands. But not kissing, embracing or touching clothed/covered private parts.
- A mother breastfeeding her baby.
### Okay - Realistic (Okay_Realistic)
- Same as above, but for realistic/photo styles.
## **Brand-Risky**
**Definition:**
Adult-oriented or sensitive, but **non-explicit**. This category is for mild romantic, sexy (non-explicit), moderate violence, or sensitive self-harm themes only. **No full nudity, explicit sexual acts, graphic violence, or direct harm promotion allowed—escalate all such to Not Brand Safe immediately.** No human bodies depicted in explicitly sexualized ways. Corresponds to Wattpad Rating: Everyone.
### Romantic - Illustrated (Romantic_Illustrated)
- Non-sexual displays of affection only, such as holding hands, hugging, embracing and kissing.
- Includes people wearing bathing/swimsuits, underwear, or lingerie.
- Shirtless, topless, or partially nude individuals are okay - as long as sexualized organs such as breasts, pubic region, or bottom are reasonably covered (either by opaque fabric, or strategically placed body parts e.g. hands).
- Similar to images found in an editorial/fashion magazine or billboard.
- Fully or partially clothed couples kissing or closely engaged couples would also fall in this category. In such cases, there should generally be no focus on - or touching of - sexualized organs or regions.
- This concept also captures works of art, sculptures or paintings that show partial or full nudity i.e. works of art that could be found at a museum (e.g. Michelangelos' David).
### Romantic - Realistic (Romantic_Realistic)
- Same as above, but for realistic/photo styles.
### Sexy - Illustrated (Sexy_Illustrated)
- Includes people wearing bathing/swimsuits, underwear, or lingerie, as well as shirtless, topless, or partially nude individuals. In this case though, sexualized organs such as breasts, pubic region, or butt are exposed (including through sheer fabric). This includes images that show:
  * Cleavage where nipple area is mostly covered. Displaying the side or partial quantity of breasts (no nipples visible).
  * Butt cheeks almost fully exposed - (as long as a thong/underwear is present).
  * Pelvic lines or partial exposure of pubic region, etc.
- Private Parts fully covered by hands or other objects.
- Partially clothed and touching themselves on their private parts (with their private parts covered) or undressing themselves.
- Naked imagery without genitals (or secondary sex characteristics like breasts with no nipples, bare bums) showing and not obviously engaged in a sex act.
- BDSM imagery:
  * Meant to titillate.
  * Collars on necks with leashes.
  * People/characters/anthropomorphic beings using whips.
  * People/characters/anthropomorphic beings chained/tied up, constrained using BDSM tools.
- Sexualized or sexually suggestive images of kissing or closely engaged couples. Sexually suggestive would mean:
  * Couples are not fully clothed i.e. in bathing/swimsuits, underwear, lingerie, or partially nude.
  * Positioning that alludes to or implies sexual or romantic activity (non-graphic).
- Sex toys that are not in use.
### Sexy - Realistic (Sexy_Realistic)
- Same as above, but for realistic/photo styles.
### Violence - Illustrated - Mature (Violence_Illustrated_Mature)
- Violent Imagery that would be seen on a news program today with an advisory message.
- Rating similar to TV-14 rating (Parents Strongly Cautioned): Some material may be inappropriate for children under 18 years of age.
### Violence - Realistic - Mature (Violence_Realistic_Mature)
- Same as above, but for realistic/photo styles.
### Self Harm - Illustrated - Everyone (SelfHarm_Illustrated_Everyone)
- Images of just the tools, such as a bloody razor/knife but in the context of a self-harm, suicide, or recovery story.
- Rating similar to TV-PG (Parental Guidance Suggested): Content may include moderate violence.
### Self Harm - Realistic - Everyone (SelfHarm_Realistic_Everyone)
- Same as above, but for realistic/photo styles.
## **Not Brand Safe**
**Definition:**
Any content with explicit sexual elements, pornographic, graphic violence, severe harm, illegal, or severely harmful material. **All explicit sexual content, even artistic or implied, goes here with high confidence.** Includes all forms of full nudity, explicit acts, graphic violence, or direct self-harm depictions, regardless of style or intent. Corresponds to Wattpad Rating: Banned.
### CSEM (Child Sexual Exploitation Material)
* Any sexualized content involving minors (real, depicted, or child-like figures).
* Includes any nudity, erotic posing, suggestive framing, or child-like figures in adult/sexual contexts. If age is ambiguous, default to CSEM.
### Explicit - Illustrated - Porn (Explicit_Illustrated_Porn)
- Hands touching private parts in any manner.
- Explicit depiction of sexual acts without any plot.
- Clear sex acts: meaning that while certain private parts may or may not be visible, it’s obvious the imagery is displaying sexual intercourse or any other sex act.
- Pornographic images or drawings, engaging in sexual acts.
- Full nudity - the complete exposure of any/all private parts. This includes displaying male/female genitalia, breasts and butts.
- Images containing excrement or ejaculate.
- Touching Private parts even though clothes are being worn (e.g. hands inside underwear).
- Seeing Private parts through clothing (an erect penis, breasts/nipples).
- Images of Sex Toys in use.
- Characters having sexual relations with animals or objects.
- Sexual relations/graphic nudity between anime, animals and anthropomorphic beings.
### Explicit - Realistic - Porn (Explicit_Realistic_Porn)
- Same as above, but for realistic/photo styles.
### Violence - Illustrated - Banned (Violence_Illustrated_Banned)
- Triggering Imagery of a violent act i.e: Beheading, stabbing, guns, shooting, jumping off a building, anything that glorifies killing, violence.
- Glorifying violence in any manner.
- Visual signs of blood, guts, gore.
- Imagery of abusive or violent relationship (e.g. a person being hit, kicked, branded etc.).
### Violence - Realistic - Banned (Violence_Realistic_Banned)
- Same as above, but for realistic/photo styles.
### Self Harm - Illustrated - Banned (SelfHarm_Illustrated_Banned)
- Any visual depiction of self-harm such as images of cutting or cuts on wrists, hanging, suicide etc.
### Self Harm - Realistic - Banned (SelfHarm_Realistic_Banned)
- Same as above, but for realistic/photo styles.

# **Evaluation Rules**
* **Brand Safe = strictly G-rated, zero sexual cues or ambiguities.** If any doubt, drop Brand Safe confidence to near zero and boost Not Brand Safe.
* **If any explicit sexual intent, cue, element, or possible interpretation is detected, always categorize as Not Brand Safe with high confidence (e.g., 0.95+), and low confidence (e.g., <0.05) for Brand Safe/Brand-Risky.**
* **Brand-Risky is only for confirmed non-explicit sensitive themes; scan rigorously for hidden explicit elements (e.g., in backgrounds, symbols) and override to Not Brand Safe if found.**
* **Explicit sex acts, genitalia, full nudity, graphic violence, or direct harm depictions = always Not Brand Safe with maximum confidence.**
* **If ambiguous, especially regarding sexuality, violence, or intent = Not Brand Safe with high confidence.**
* **Age guardrail:** If any sexual element + apparent minor → CSEM (Not Brand Safe). If age uncertain, assume minor if child-like features present; classify as Not Brand Safe regardless.
* **Be extremely conservative: prioritize false positives for Not Brand Safe over false negatives for Brand Safe/Brand-Risky. For any human body depiction, check for sexualization multiple times (e.g., pose, clothing, focus, context). Determine if image is illustrated (drawn/cartoon) or realistic (photo-like) to assign sub-rating.**
* **Common pitfalls to avoid:** Do not classify artistic full nudity as Brand-Risky if explicit; always Not Brand Safe. Do not allow swimwear or lingerie in Brand Safe if there's any body focus or exposure. Do not misinterpret "romantic" as non-sexual if poses suggest eroticism. Ignore artistic merit—focus on content rules.
* **Multi-step analysis:** 1. Detect all elements (bodies, poses, clothing, text) and style (illustrated vs realistic). 2. Check for sexual, violent, or harm cues. 3. Apply rules conservatively. 4. Assign confidences based on evidence.
