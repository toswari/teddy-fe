# **Taxonomy**
## **Safe**
**Definition:**
Completely **G-rated, universally appropriate, and workplace-safe.** Absolutely no sexual content, cues, or interpretations possible. No violence or harm themes.
Allowed:
* Landscapes, animals, abstract art without any human or animal forms that could be sexualized.
* Everyday non-sexual depictions of fully clothed people (all ages) in neutral activities (e.g., walking, reading).
* Emotional but non-sexual gestures (hugging, crying, hand-holding) with no body emphasis or close contact that could imply intimacy.
* Swimwear in public/family-friendly contexts **only if fully modest (e.g., one-piece, no cleavage or tight fits), no posing, and no focus on body curves, skin, or outlines**.
* Breastfeeding shown as nurturing, discreet, and non-sexual (no visible nipple/areola, no skin exposure beyond minimal arm/neck, no emphasis on breasts or body; e.g., fully covered with blanket).
Excluded (must escalate to Banned):
* Any nudity or partial nudity beyond the strict breastfeeding allowance above.
* Any suggestion of erotic/sexual cues (e.g., lingerie, seductive posing, pin-up styling, tight clothing with emphasis, even if non-explicit).
* Any human forms or poses that could be misinterpreted as sensual (e.g., arched backs, lingering gazes, parted lips).
* Violence, self-harm, or any harm themes (escalate to Mature or Banned based on intensity).
* Anything remotely ambiguous in a sexual context, including cultural or artistic interpretations.

## **Mature**
**Definition:**
Adult-oriented or sensitive, but **strictly non-sexual**. This category is for non-sexual mature themes like moderate violence or emotional distress only. **No nudity, sexual cues, or erotic elements allowed—escalate all such to Banned immediately.** No human bodies depicted in ways that could be sexualized.
### Violence (Moderate)
* Fantasy/comic battles or fights (no gore, no blood, no realistic harm).
* Injuries depicted without blood/gore, graphic detail, or promotion of violence.
### Self-Harm (Sensitive)
* Symbolic/metaphorical depictions of distress (e.g., abstract art of sadness, without methods or wounds).
* Healed scars or recovery narratives without depicting methods, active wounds, or encouragement.
* **Never depict methods, active wounds, encouragement, or graphic elements.**
Escalate to **Banned** if:
* Any sexual, nudity, or erotic elements are present (override all confidences).
* Violence becomes graphic, realistic, or promotes harm.
* Self-harm is explicit or instructional.

## **Banned**
**Definition:**
Any content with sexual elements (at any level), pornographic, illegal, or severely harmful material. **All potential sexual content, even mild, artistic, or implied, goes here with high confidence.** Includes all forms of nudity or eroticism, regardless of style or intent.
### CSEM (Child Sexual Exploitation Material)
* Any sexualized content involving minors (real, depicted, or child-like figures).
* Includes any nudity, erotic posing, suggestive framing, or child-like figures in adult/sexual contexts. If age is ambiguous, default to CSEM.
### Sexual/Nudity
* Any nudity in art, fashion, culture, or context (e.g., artistic nudes, topless figures, even silhouettes).
* Lingerie, boudoir, fetish aesthetics, or any clothing emphasizing sexuality (e.g., sheer fabrics, low-cut).
* Sensual posing, romantic intimacy, or any body-focused elements (even without contact or nudity).
* Sexual acts (oral sex, intercourse, masturbation, sex toys, even implied).
* Pornographic framing or intent.
* Visible/silhouetted genitalia, anus, exposed buttocks, or pubic areas in any context.
* Erotic breast nudity (nipples/areolae visible) or any breast emphasis (e.g., cleavage focus).
* Non-consensual sexual activity or implications.
* Any content with even subtle erotic or sexual intent (e.g., "sexy" outfits, flirty expressions, implied arousal).
### Violence (Graphic or Promoting Harm)
* Gore, mutilation, torture, or severe injury.
* Graphic self-harm (bleeding, cutting, suicide attempts).
* Content promoting violence, suicide, or harm.

# **Evaluation Rules**
* **Safe = strictly G-rated, zero sexual cues or ambiguities.** If any doubt, drop Safe confidence to near zero and boost Banned.
* **If any sexual intent, cue, element, or possible interpretation is detected, always categorize as Banned with high confidence (e.g., 0.95+), and low confidence (e.g., <0.05) for Safe/Mature.**
* **Mature is only for confirmed non-sexual mature themes; scan rigorously for hidden sexual elements (e.g., in backgrounds, symbols) and override to Banned if found.**
* **Sex acts, genitalia, nudity, erotic posing, or suggestive elements = always Banned with maximum confidence.**
* **If ambiguous, especially regarding sexuality or intent = Banned with high confidence.**
* **Age guardrail:** If any sexual element + apparent minor → CSEM (Banned). If age uncertain, assume minor if child-like features present; classify as Banned regardless.
* **Be extremely conservative: prioritize false positives for Banned over false negatives for Safe/Mature. For any human body depiction, check for sexualization multiple times (e.g., pose, clothing, focus, context).**
* **Common pitfalls to avoid:** Do not classify artistic nudity as Mature; always Banned. Do not allow swimwear or lingerie in Safe/Mature if there's any body focus or curve emphasis. Do not misinterpret "romantic" as non-sexual if poses suggest intimacy. Ignore artistic merit—focus on content rules.
* **Multi-step analysis:** 1. Detect all elements (bodies, poses, clothing, text). 2. Check for sexual cues. 3. Apply rules conservatively. 4. Assign confidences based on evidence.
